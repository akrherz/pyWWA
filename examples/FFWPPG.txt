National Weather Service Pago Pago AS

The National Weather Service in Pago Pago has issued a

* Flash Flood Warning for
  Tutuila Aunuu

* Damage Threat Considerable

* Until 5 AM SST

* At 202 AM, Rain gauge indicated heavy rainfall near NWS Pago
  Pago. Flash flooding is imminent or already occurring.

  HAZARD...Flash flooding caused by heavy rain.

  SOURCE...Rain gauge indicated over four inches of rainfall in
the past two hours at NWS Pago Pago. Additional heavy rainfall is
expected over the coming hours.

  IMPACT...Significant and life threatening flooding in
           drainages, streams, rivers, roads, properties
           and other low lying areas. Multiple road
           closures expected, as well as landslides
           in steep terrain.

PRECAUTIONARY/PREPAREDNESS ACTIONS...

A flash flood warning means that flooding is imminent or
occurring within the warned area.

The heavy rains may cause rock and mudslides in steep terrain
areas. Stay away from streams, rivers, drainage ditches, and
culverts, even if they are currently dry.

Do not cross fast flowing water in your vehicle, or on foot. Find
an alternate route. Turn around, don't drown.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION...Considerable impacts, such as flooding
of homes and widespread landslides are possible, especially in
the Tafuna - Nu'uuli area where rain gauges have measured the
heaviest rainfall accumulations.
